Our Programs

Discover comprehensive pathways designed to guide students at every stage of their Canadian journey, from high school to university and beyond.

Study in High School

Students complete 2-4 years at one of our partnered Christian high schools and graduate with the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD), equivalent to A-Level/IB qualifications.

Who It's For

International students seeking a complete high school experience in Canada with Christian values and comprehensive support.

Timeline

2-4 years | Semester 1 begins September

Apply Now

What's Included

Student accommodation (our own — a home away from home)
Birthday & holiday celebrations
Medical appointment accompaniment
Health card, banking, SIN assistance
Driving school enrollment support
First Aid certification
Ongoing parent communication

Look See Canada

Who It's For

Students considering studying in Canada who want to experience Canadian life firsthand.

Timeline

Look See Africa

We invite students to spend 6-8 weeks with us in Africa — not just visiting, but truly experiencing daily life across this beautiful continent. This program is designed for families who want their child to explore Africa's rich cultural heritage and natural wonders before committing to long-term study abroad.

Who It's For

Students considering studying in Africa who want to experience African life, culture, and education firsthand.

Timeline

6-8 weeks | Multiple intakes (June-August & December-January)

Destinations

South Africa, Kenya, Ghana, Morocco, Rwanda, and Botswana

Apply Now

Activities & Experiences Include

Safari wildlife experiences (Big 5 viewing)
Cultural village visits & traditional ceremonies
Swahili language & drumming workshops
Community service & conservation projects
Table Mountain hiking & coastal exploration
African music, dance & storytelling
Victoria Falls adventure activities
Local school visits & cultural exchange
African cuisine cooking classes

Program Highlights

Experience the warmth of African hospitality, learn from local elders, and gain a deeper understanding of Africa's diverse cultures, all while making lifelong friendships.
