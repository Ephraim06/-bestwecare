About Us

We're on a mission to transform the international student experience through faith-based mentorship, structured support, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Story

Moving to a new country as a young student can be overwhelming, especially when arriving alone. Choosing to send your child to Canada is a major decision — academically, emotionally, and spiritually. We understand the weight of that responsibility.

At @BestWeCare, our mission is to gather, grow, guide, and release students into their full potential. We support international students who want to study in Canada by providing a soft and safe landing into the Canadian education system.

Starting in high school, we introduce them to the system early to help them overcome social barriers, navigate culture shock, and build confidence. We prepare our students for long-term success well before university begins.

Beverley Allen

Director of Programs

Beverly Allen serves as the Director of Programs at @BestWeCare Inc., bringing professional expertise in child and youth development, behavioural guidance, and holistic student development.

She holds an Ontario College Advanced Diploma in Child and Youth Worker from Mohawk College and is certified in Prevention and Management of Aggressive Behaviour (PMAB).
