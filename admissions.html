Admissions Process

Your journey to studying in Canada starts here. Follow our structured admissions process for a smooth transition.

Application Timeline

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form through our official portal. Ensure all information is accurate and complete.

Timeline: 1-2 days

2

Pay Application Fee

Submit the non-refundable application fee of $180 USD. Fee must be paid before document review begins.

Timeline: Immediate

3

Document Review

Submit academic transcripts, passport copy, and English proficiency test scores (if applicable). Our team reviews all documents for completeness.

Timeline: 5-7 business days

4

Zoom Interview

Meet with our admissions team for a personal interview. This helps us understand your goals and ensure you're the right fit for our programs.

Timeline: Scheduled within 1 week

5

Admission Decision

Receive notification of acceptance. Successful candidates receive an offer letter with program details.

Timeline: 3-5 business days after interview

6

Enrollment & Visa Process

Complete enrollment forms, pay tuition deposit, and begin visa application process with our guidance.

Timeline: 4-8 weeks for visa processing

Application Requirements

Valid Passport

Copy of valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond intended stay.

Academic Transcripts

Official transcripts from last 2-3 years of study, translated to English if necessary.

English Proficiency

IELTS, TOEFL, or equivalent scores (if applicable to your program).

Statement of Purpose

Personal essay outlining your goals, reasons for choosing Canada, and alignment with our values.

Reference Letters

Two reference letters from teachers or mentors who can speak to your character and academic ability.

Medical Insurance

Proof of medical insurance coverage for your stay in Canada (can be arranged after acceptance).

Selection Process

Please note that admission is competitive. We follow a rigorous and structured selection process to ensure we admit students who are academically prepared, committed, aligned with our values, and the best fit for our schools and us.

Our goal is not simply placement, but the right placement. We carefully assess readiness, character, and long-term potential to ensure each student is positioned for success.

Competition is strong, and spaces are limited. We encourage families to apply early and prepare thoroughly.
