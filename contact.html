Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

The complete application process typically takes 4-6 weeks from submission to final decision, depending on document verification and interview scheduling.

What are the English language requirements?

Requirements vary by program. Generally, we accept IELTS (6.0+), TOEFL (80+), or equivalent. Some programs may have alternative requirements.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa guidance and document support. However, final visa approval is at the discretion of Canadian immigration authorities.
